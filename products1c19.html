Product ID: WEB-DGV-2VC
Weber 32/36 DGV-2V Carburetor

Details 

Perfect for stock or mild performance engines, the Weber 32/36 DGV carb is a progressive down-draft carburetor with counter-clockwise rotation. As a progressive carb it operates as a one barrel most of the time, with the second barrel only opening when more power is needed, or under wide open throttle. While it comes with a manual choke, electric and hot water chokes are available (see options). The DGV offers increased performance, trouble-free maintenance, excellent drivability, and in most cases, improved fuel economy. See Details for more information.

Note: Occasionally the carb will work fine out of the box, however in most cases you'll need to rejet the carb to get the best performance. Please see our tech article on Adjustments & Tuning for more information.
NOTE: Failure to purge the gas line and/or installing a new fuel filter will void the warranty.
