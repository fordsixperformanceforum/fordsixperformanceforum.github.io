Product ID: WEB-DGV-SWP-K
Weber "Carb Swap" Kit

Details 

Save $20 dollars 
Our "Carb Swap" Kit comes with a carb adaptor, a fuel pressure regulator or an electric fuel pump,  universal linkage kit, and a Weber two barrel carb with a manual choke (electric or hot water choke optional). Air cleaners sold separately. See DETAILS for more information. 

Note: No modifications are required to the log manifold, you just need to verify the size of the manifold before you order. The small log manifold has a 1.5" carb bore (hole), while the large log manifold has a 1.75" carb bore.
Note: You can use your stock fuel pump with a fuel pressure regulator, or an electric fuel pump (see options). If you use the AirTex Electric Fuel Pump, you do not need a fuel pressure regulator. A stainless steel Fuel Pump Block-off Plate is optional.
Note: In most cases you'll need to replace your stock distributor with an aftermarket distributor, such as a Duraspark II or DUI distributor and/or ignition kit. Please reaad our tech articles on Ignition System Choices and Load-O-Matic Distributors for more information.
Note: Modifications to the carb linkage are required. Some guys modify their existing linkage, while others install a throttle cable. These can be purchased new from Lokar, or you can adapt a used pedal and cable from a bone yard donor car, such as a Maverick or Granada.
Note: Occasionally the carb will work fine out of the box, however in most cases you'll need to rejet the carb to get the best performance. Please see our tech article on Adjustments & Tuning for more information.
NOTE: Failure to purge the gas line and/or installing a new fuel filter will void the warranty.
